Blooming Cinnamon Rolls

Bring a fresh dish to family brunch with this recipe that's indulgent and deliciously sweet.

This recipe turns our Flaky Cinnamon Rolls into an ooey-gooey, family-pleasing, pull-apart roll that looks like a flower in full bloom. From the baked sugary sprinkled tops to the cut grooves that soak up sweet icing and caramel sauce, this is one roll that’s unforgettable. Make with your little chefs, and enjoy every bite together.

Ingredients

  • 1 can (17.5 oz) refrigerated Pillsbury™ Grands!™ Flaky Cinnamon Rolls with Original Icing (5 Count)
  • 1 tablespoon butter, melted
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 5 tablespoons caramel topping or caramel sauce

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 350°F. Line 9-inch round cake pan with cooking parchment paper.
  2. Separate dough into 5 rounds; reserve icing. Using a kitchen scissors, make cuts around the top inside swirl of each cinnamon roll (do not cut outer swirl of dough), about an inch apart and halfway down (not all the way to the bottom).
  3. Brush tops with melted butter; sprinkle with sugar.
  4. Bake 23 to 28 minutes or until golden brown. In small microwavable bowl, microwave icing uncovered on High 5 to 10 seconds, stirring until thin enough to drizzle.
  5. Drizzle about 2 teaspoons of the icing on top of each cinnamon roll. Top each with 1 tablespoon of the caramel sauce, carefully spreading on top and into the cut grooves of hot cinnamon rolls. Serve with remaining icing.
